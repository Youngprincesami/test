DIY Home Décor Ideas on a Budget

You don't need to spend a fortune to decorate your home beautifully. Here are some creative DIY ideas that won’t break the bank.


  1. Upcycled Furniture

    Give old furniture a new life with a coat of paint or new upholstery.

  2. Homemade Wall Art

    Create your own art pieces using canvas, paint, and personal photos.

  3. Natural Elements

    Use branches, stones, and plants to bring nature indoors.

  4. Decorative Pillows

    Sew or decorate throw pillows for a pop of color in your living space.

  5. Gallery Wall

    Display your favorite photos or artwork in a gallery wall layout.

Conclusion

With a little creativity and effort, you can transform your space without spending a lot of money.
